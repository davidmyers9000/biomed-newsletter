Always Sunny in Philadelphia

Dear All:

The College of Engineering and the School of Biomedical
Engineering, Science and Health Systems will be hosting an
Inaugural Joint Open House to showcase Drexel’s innovation and
collaboration in engineering research and graduate studies. This
novel event will include an overview of Master’s and Doctoral
programs, brief introduction to members of our faculty and their
research, an opportunity to meet with current students and
admissions staff, as well as laboratory and campus tours.

The event will take place on Friday, April 6, 2018
starting at 10 AM in the Bossone Research Enterprise Center
located at 3140 Market Street, Philadelphia, PA 19104.

All prospective and accepted students interested in the
following disciplines are invited to attend:

* Architectural Engineering
* Biomedical Engineering and Biomedical Science
* Chemical Engineering
* Civil Engineering
* Computer Engineering
* Electrical Engineering
* Engineering Technology
* Environmental Engineering
* Materials Science and Engineering
* Mechanical Engineering

information/attend.
