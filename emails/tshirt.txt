The School of Biomedical Engineering, Science and
Health Systems invites incoming and current students to
participate in its T-shirt Design Contest. Two winning designs
will be selected by a panel of judges, and students will vote on
their favorite via Facebook. The final winning design will be
turned into merchandise, and prizes will be awarded to creators
of both final designs.

-------------------
Design Requirements
-------------------

To be eligible for consideration in the contest, each
design must:

* use 2 color maximum; design can appear on the shirt’s
front, back or both (the shirt itself will be navy)
* incorporate biomedical engineering (as a discipline), Mario the
Dragon (the University’s mascot), and the School’s full name (The
School of Biomedical Engineering, Science and Health Systems at
Drexel University) (Game of Thrones inspired designs are
welcomed)
* not be larger than 15”X15”
* appeal to the broadest possible audience
