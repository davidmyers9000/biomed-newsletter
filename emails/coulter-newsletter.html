  • Orthopedic Surgical Devices with Controlled Fluid- Induced Expansion Properties for Improved Bone Fixation and Bone Integration

    Sorin Siegler, PhD, Giuseppe Palmese, PhD, Antonios Kontsos, PhD, Pablo Huang Zhang, PhD, Keith Wapner, MD, Christopher Jones, MD, Michael Ciccotti, MD, Jarrett Cain, DPM, Thomas P. Shaer, VMD, and Brian Garvey
