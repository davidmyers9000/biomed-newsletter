2020-21 Coulter Awardees
------------------------

The Coulter-Drexel Translational Research Partnership
Program has awarded over $880,000 total for 2020-2021 to the
following projects.

* Mxene Sorbents for Efficient and Cost-Effective
Dialysate Regeneration

Yury Gogotsi, PhD, Meera Harhay, MD, Mykola Seredych,
PhD, Jesse Kim, and Ian G. Welsford

* Preclinical optimization of a novel neuroprotective agent to
treat Parkinson’s Disease

Sandhya Kortagere, PhD and Jill Farmer, D.O, MPH

* Use of non-thermal plasma for treatment of HSV infections

Vandana Miller, MD, Fred Krebs, PhD, Stephen
Jennings, PhD, Brian Wigdahl, PhD, Sean Wang, PhD, and Ken Li,
PhD

* Megabase DNA Sequencing System

Ming Xiao, PhD, Dharma Varapula, Lahari Uppuluri,
Moses Noh, PhD

* Team IEP: A cloud application for urban districts to improve
transition outcomes for ASD youth through iterative,
collaborative and synchronized implementation of transition goals
by youth, parents and school staff

Elizabeth McGhee Hassrick, PhD, Brigid Garvin, Ed.S.
NCSP, Bridget Sweeney Blakely, PhD, Troy Finamore, Andrew
Stutzman, and Paul Phan

* Orthopedic Surgical Devices with Controlled Fluid- Induced
Expansion Properties for Improved Bone Fixation and Bone
Integration

Sorin Siegler, PhD, Giuseppe Palmese, PhD, Antonios
Kontsos, PhD, Pablo Huang Zhang, PhD, Keith Wapner, MD,
